About Us

Creations are the heat of fire, the blooming of flowers, and the start of our firework.
Hello everyone, we are Hanabi!
This website is dedicated to spreading and sparking inspiration between artists and creators everywhere.
All creations are welcome, whether they are bold as flames, or delicate as petals.
Hanabi means fireworks, and we offer you the fireworks of creation.

Some things you should know:

- There is no search bar
- Commenting is disabled
- Entering Contests is not possible

Why is all this, you may ask?

Well, in order to create this website, we used the coding languages of CSS and HTML.
These two 'languages' are only two of the many used 'languages' used to make websites.
Different languages are used to add different parts of the site.
In order to make a search bar, for example, we need to use JavaScript, a language I do not know.
Because of this, several things are not able to work.

Sorry for the inconvenience!!
