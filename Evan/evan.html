Shoe-line Skate art

A single hockey skate out of a pair, the shoe is beautifully lined and coloured by the creator of it; Evan Elzbet.
The piece uses a unique combination of lines as well as the eye-catching usage of the two different colours - Blue and yellow, that together, create a well paired duo that go splendidly well with the sharpness of the skate.
These colours are a successful representation of the creator's hockey team, as stated by him.
